Mantle Jump Easy

A Superglide needs a jump input first and then a crouch input 1 frame later. You need to do the whole Superglide in the last 0.15 second of a mantle. That makes the correct timing of Jump -> Crouch way harder than timing the whole Superglide in the mantle. This trainer will help you learn that much harder Jump -> Crouch timing.

Map 2 by DeaFPS

The geyser mega jump can be done at any of the geysers on worlds edge. By wedging your character between the rocks and the geyser then walking into the geyser, it will send you flying. Most often done by putting yourself into a forced crouch position. Holding crouch and not crouching will both give you different results in distance and trajectory
