Elite Jump

An elite jump is essentially a super jump redirect on a zipline. It gives more height than a typical superjump tap strafe and you have likely seen clips of it in any zip building.

Jitter Peek - New Castle Wall

This is a variation of gorilla peeking where you spam mantle cancels, however on a new castle wall because it pushes you off you dont need to cancel your mantles and can just spam your forward key creating this strange peek.

Mantle Peek

The geyser mega jump can be done at any of the geysers on worlds edge. By wedging your character between the rocks and the geyser then walking into the geyser, it will send you flying. Most often done by putting yourself into a forced crouch position. Holding crouch and not crouching will both give you different results in distance and trajectory

Pito Strafe

The Pito strafe is a basic lurch often used to close distance or avoid damage. It uses A+W+D for a forward pito strafe, and can also be reversed.

Ultra Jump

Ultra jump is higher than both a hyper and super jump. By setting two ziplines that overlap and superjumping off of them you can get the insane Ultra Jump
