9 Types of Walljumps


This video covers 9 different wallbounce techniques: Slide Jump, Fatigue, Ghost-strafe, Crouch, Standing, Coyote, Skip Jump

Jumpad Lurching

You can combine your lurches with jumpads, When you go off a pad time your lurch with your jump/second jump. This allows you to not have to move your camera in order to turn but isnt noted as an incredibly helpful tech.

  • Tutorial

Coyote Wallbounce

Coyote Jumps are one of few options for wall bouncing immediately after a climb or when you have limited space. - JayTheYggdrasil

  • Tutorial

Mantle Peek

The geyser mega jump can be done at any of the geysers on worlds edge. By wedging your character between the rocks and the geyser then walking into the geyser, it will send you flying. Most often done by putting yourself into a forced crouch position. Holding crouch and not crouching will both give you different results in distance and trajectory
